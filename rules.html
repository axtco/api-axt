Constitution

Strict adherence to these rules ensures a high-quality roleplay environment for everyone. Ignorance is not an excuse.

General Conduct
Respect & Toxicity
Zero Tolerance

We are a community first. Racism, homophobia, sexism, or any form of OOC (Out of Character) harassment will result in a permanent ban.

Microphone Required
General

A working, decent quality microphone is mandatory. Text-based RP is not permitted on this server.

Exploiting / Hacking
Perm Ban

Using crosshairs, mod menus, duplicating items, or abusing game mechanics (glitches) to gain an advantage is strictly prohibited.

Advertising
General

Do not advertise other servers or communities within AxtCity (In-game or Discord).

Roleplay Standards
RDM (Random Death Match)
Combat

Attacking another player without a valid roleplay reason or prior initiation. You must interact verbally before engaging in combat.

Violation: Walking up to a stranger and shooting them because "they looked at you wrong" without saying a word.
VDM (Vehicle Death Match)
Combat

Using your vehicle as a weapon to run over or ram other players/vehicles without a valid RP reason.

NVL (No Value of Life)
Immersion

You must value your character's life realistically. If you have a gun pointed at you, you must comply (unless you have a realistic opportunity to counter).

Violation: Running away or pulling out your own weapon while you already have a gun to your head.
Metagaming
Immersion

Using Out-of-Character (OOC) information in-game. This includes stream sniping or using Discord info for IC advantage.

Powergaming
Immersion

Forcing roleplay on others without giving them a chance to respond, or doing things that are physically impossible.

Violation: Using /me puts handcuffs on person (without giving them a chance to resist).
New Life Rule (NLR)
Immersion

If you are downed and respawn at the hospital (MD), you forget the events leading up to your death. You cannot return to the scene of death for 15 minutes.

Zones & Interactions
Green Zones
Safety

No crime, fighting, or stealing in Green Zones. These include: Hospitals, Police Stations, Government Buildings, and Clothing Stores.

Combat Logging
Bannable

Disconnecting from the server to avoid roleplay situations (being arrested, kidnapped, or downed) is strictly prohibited.

Rules are subject to change. Check Discord for the latest updates.
