Play Behind Reality

Step into a world where every citizen has a role, every decision matters, and every day brings new opportunities to create your own story.

Our Origins

AxtCity was born from the vision of passionate roleplay creators who wanted a place where imagination meets reality. From humble beginnings, it has grown into a sprawling metropolis full of life, adventure, and countless possibilities.

Our Vision

Our goal is to provide an immersive, dynamic roleplay experience where players can explore careers, build narratives, and shape the city around them. We strive for a balance of serious roleplay and genuine fun.

The City

From bustling downtown streets to quiet suburban neighborhoods, AxtCity is a city of contrasts. Each district has its own culture, challenges, and opportunities, giving players the freedom to craft unique stories.

The Community

AxtCity thrives on creativity, collaboration, and respect. Our community is the heartbeat of the city, welcoming both new roleplayers and veterans who contribute to the evolving story of the city.
