AxtCity Citizen's Code of Conduct

Authority: Mr. X and Associates Type: Official IC Statute

Preamble: Authority and Order

This Code represents the foundational legal framework of AxtCity. Every citizen, resident, and visitor is bound by these articles. The enforcement of this Code, including judicial and correctional measures, falls under the exclusive jurisdiction of the AxtCity Authority, represented by Mr. X and Associates.

1. Civic Duties and Identification

Obligations required of every individual to maintain civil order and functional governance.

1.1 Mandatory Identification (ID)

  • Every individual within AxtCity jurisdiction must carry valid, government-issued photo identification at all times.
  • Failure to produce identification upon request by an official authority (Police, Authority Representative) constitutes a violation of Article 1.1.
  • Possession of counterfeit or multiple identification documents is a serious felony offense.
Penalty: Failure to Identify - Citation and immediate detention for record verification. Counterfeit ID - Felony charge and Asset Seizure.

1.2 Compliance with Authority

  • Citizens must comply immediately and non-verbally with all lawful commands issued by authorized Law Enforcement or AxtCity Authority personnel.
  • Evasion of detention or physical resistance to arrest, regardless of the initial charge, escalates all charges to Felony Evasion.

2. Public Order and Safety

Regulations governing traffic, weapons, and general conduct in public spaces.

2.1 Traffic and Vehicle Code

  • All vehicles must be legally registered with the AxtCity Vehicle Registry. Operating an unregistered vehicle is an offense.
  • Reckless endangerment through vehicular maneuvers (excessive speed in city limits, aggressive swerving, intentional collision) is strictly prohibited.
  • Hit and Run involving property damage or injury is a Felony offense and results in immediate revocation of the driving license.

2.2 Public Carry of Weapons

  • Possession of firearms in public areas requires a valid Firearm Permit issued by the AxtCity Authority.
  • Carrying concealed weapons without a specific concealed carry permit is prohibited.
  • Use of any weapon to intimidate, threaten, or injure another citizen without clear self-defense justification is an aggravated assault felony.
Penalty: Public Weapons Violation - Confiscation of weapon, heavy fine, and potential prison sentence.

3. Business and Licensing

Rules regarding commercial activity, taxation, and asset management within AxtCity.

3.1 Operating Licenses

  • All commercial enterprises (including street vendors and service providers) must possess a valid, renewed AxtCity Business License.
  • Any large-scale financial transaction, property sale, or corporate merger must be registered with and approved by the AxtCity Authority Financial Oversight Board.

3.2 Financial Crime

  • Failure to pay required city taxes or fees by the annual deadline constitutes Tax Evasion.
  • Unlicensed manufacturing, distribution, or sale of prohibited goods (contraband, illegal narcotics, unregistered equipment) is deemed Organized Crime, a high-level felony.
  • Money laundering or misrepresentation of asset origin is strictly illegal and subject to full asset seizure.

4. Enforcement & Penalties

The consequences for breaches of the AxtCity Code.

4.1 Categorization of Charges

  • Minor Citation: Traffic violations, failure to display valid tags, minor disturbances. (Fine only).
  • Misdemeanor: Petty theft, resisting minor compliance, simple assault. (Fine + Short Jail Sentence).
  • Felony: Aggravated assault, large-scale theft, illegal weapon use, evasion of authority, all financial crimes. (Heavy Fine + Extended Jail Sentence, possible Asset Seizure).

4.2 Authority to Detain

  • Law Enforcement and Authority Personnel are authorized to use proportionate force, including restraints, to detain and secure subjects in violation of this Code.
  • The final determination of charges and sentencing is performed by an Authority representative upon processing.

Mandate of Adherence

This document is law. Ignorance of the AxtCity Code provides no exemption from prosecution. All citizens are required to conduct themselves in accordance with these statutes.
